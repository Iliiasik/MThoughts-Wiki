# Overview

**Midnight Thoughts** expands the sleep experience in Minecraft, transforming sleep from a passive skip mechanic into a meaningful gameplay system.

<Admonition type="info" title="Core idea">
    Sleep is no longer just a way to skip the night — it becomes part of progression, storytelling, and competition.
</Admonition>

---

## Goal

The goal of the mod is to make sleep an active gameplay mechanic rather than a passive action.

Instead of simply skipping the night, sleep becomes:

-  a storytelling system through dreams and nightmares
-  a progression system with buffs and rewards
-  a social competition system between players

This encourages players to go to bed not just for survival, but for **progress, interaction, and competition**.
